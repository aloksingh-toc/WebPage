How It's Made

Our Manufacturing Process

Raw Material Selection

We source only virgin PE100 and PE80 grade resins from globally reputed suppliers. Every batch undergoes Melt Flow Index (MFI) testing and density verification before being approved for production.

Resin Sourcing MFI Testing Carbon Black Mixing

Precision Extrusion

Our state-of-the-art single-screw extruders maintain precise temperature and pressure control. The molten HDPE compound passes through an annular die to form the pipe profile with exact dimensional accuracy.

Temperature Control Annular Die Forming Wall Thickness Check

Vacuum Cooling

Extruded pipes pass through a vacuum sizing tank and spray cooling baths to achieve uniform wall thickness and roundness. This controlled cooling prevents internal stress and warpage.

Vacuum Sizing Spray Cooling Roundness Check

Marking & Printing

Each pipe is inkjet-printed with product details including diameter, pressure rating, material grade, batch number, BIS license, and manufacturing date for complete traceability.

Inkjet Printing BIS License Mark Batch Tracing

Quality Testing

Every production lot undergoes rigorous testing - hydrostatic pressure testing, OIT, tensile strength, impact resistance, and dimensional verification - before receiving BIS certification.

Hydrostatic Test Tensile Strength Impact Resistance

Coiling & Packing

Smaller diameter pipes are coiled using automatic coiling machines while larger diameters are cut to standard 6m/12m lengths. All products are strapped and palletized for safe transport.

Auto Coiling Length Cutting Palletization
